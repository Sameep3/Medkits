About Us

Hands of Hope is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to creating sustainable solutions to poverty and inequity. We partner with local leaders, volunteers, and donors to deliver programs that meet critical needs.

Our Vision

A community where student-led action creates a lasting impact, ensuring that no one in the Greater Salt Lake Area shelters goes without the basic medical and hygiene care they deserve.

Our Story

Hands of Hope is a student-led organization made up of high schoolers from Bingham High School, Corner Canyon High School, and Hillcrest High School in South Jordan, Utah. We came together after recognizing a growing need around the Greater Salt Lake Area—people experiencing homelessness without access to even the most basic necessities like medical kits, blankets, and hygiene supplies.

We saw that local shelters are doing their best, but many struggle to keep up due to limited resources. As students, we knew we couldn’t ignore the problem—so we decided to take action.

So we created a student-run nonprofit to help fill that gap.

What started as a small idea between friends is now a growing effort led entirely by high school students who are committed to making a difference—right here at home. We believe that even though we’re young, we can take real action and bring our community together to support those who need it most.
