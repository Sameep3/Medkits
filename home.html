Our Story

A student-led initiative to bring hope and essential supplies to the most vulnerable in our community.

Hands of Hope is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization made up of high schoolers from Bingham, Corner Canyon, and Hillcrest High Schools. We came together after recognizing a critical need: people experiencing homelessness in the Greater Salt Lake Area often lack access to basic medical and hygiene supplies.

While local shelters work tirelessly, their resources are stretched thin. As students, we felt a responsibility to act. What started as a small idea is now a growing, student-run movement committed to making a tangible difference, right here at home.

What We Provide

We focus on delivering essential kits that provide comfort, health, and hope.

Medical Kits

Basic first-aid supplies like bandages, antiseptic wipes, and pain relievers to treat minor injuries and prevent infection.

Hygiene Kits

Toothbrushes, toothpaste, soap, and other personal care items to maintain health and restore dignity.

Community Outreach

Partnering with local shelters and organizations to distribute kits and connect individuals with further resources.
